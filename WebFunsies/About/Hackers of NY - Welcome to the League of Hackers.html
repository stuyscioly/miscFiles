The Hackers movement was started with Hackers of NY in Feb 2014, but has now grown to five cities worldwide: Hackers of SV, Hackers of LA, Hackers of London, Hackers of Chicago and of course the original NY.

The League of Hackers was founded by Dani Grant (NY + SV) and grown by the incredible work of Steven Chan (NY), Terri Burns (LA), Tosin Afolabi (LDN) and Niraj Pant (CHI)

If you’d like to start a League of Hackers site in your own city, just holler.
